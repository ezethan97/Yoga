What is Bikram Yoga?

Bikram Yoga is a system of hot yoga consisting of a fixed sequence of 26 postures and 2 breathing exercises, practised in a heated and humid room.

Who am I?

My name is Tu Nguyen. As a certified Bikram Yoga teacher for the last 17 years I have had the privilege of sharing the world's most popular yoga practice around the globe; from New Zealand and Australia, across Europe, South America, southeast Asia, Canada and the US and most recently in the place of my birth, Louisiana. I am so excited to bring the Bikram practice to my homeland and especially my adopted home of An Bang Beach Village. I look forward to sharing this life changing practice with you and welcoming you into my home and what I hope will become your yoga studio while you are here in this wonderful country.
